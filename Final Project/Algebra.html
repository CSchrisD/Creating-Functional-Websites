Equations

An equation is like a statement that can be read from left to right like a sentence. It determines if something is equal to another, and usually there is a number of variables on either side of the equals sign. Variables can be represented by any letter, but usually they are represented by x and y.


ax + b = cy + d

Formulas

It is a special equation in which a relationship among variables is defined. Area and volume are formulas because they have variables defined to have known lengths placed in to find a resulting size.


A well-known example of a formula is the quadratic formula, which is used to factor equations.
