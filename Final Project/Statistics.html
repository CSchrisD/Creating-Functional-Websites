Mean, Median, Mode, and Range

Mean

Mean, also known as average, is a sum of all values divided by the total numbers summed.

Median

Arrange the series of numbers in order. If the series is even in size the median is the center most number. If the series is odd the median is the two middle numbers sum divided by 2.
